Introduction

The aim of this MSc is to provide students with the necessary computational skills to effectively manage and interpret biomedical (big) data. It is lead by the Genomic Resources research group of the University of Basque Country (UPV/EHU).

The MSc will be taught 2015-2016, and it is under evaluation for the (very likely) UPV/EHU release. It is a "EHU own degree".

Entry requirements

Students of scientific and technical backgrounds are encouraged to apply. The first half of the programme is divided to provide the appropriate tuition to each student, according to their background: students with a technical background (Computer science, engineering, physics, ...) should choose the Life Sciences path, whereas students with a biomedical background (Biologists, Medical Doctors, biochemists, environmentalists, ... ) should choose the Computer Science path. See programme for details. Students should be fluent in english (the tuition will be provided exclusively in english) and will be selected according to their CVs.
